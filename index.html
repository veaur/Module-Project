Titration

Titation is a technique where a solution of known concentration is used to determine the concentration of an unkown solution.

Things you would need to titrate are

  • Buret
  • White Paper
  • Volumetric pipet and pupet bulb
  • pH indecator
  • Erlenmeyer flask
  • A solution of known concentration
  • A solution of unkown concentration
  • Wash bottle
  • Beaker

Acid and Bases

Acids and Bases are key fundemenelts of Chem 30 and you will need to know the pHs of them all!

An Acid is a substance with a pH lower than 7 like HCl!
A base is a substance with a pH higher than 7 like NaOH!
A substance that has a pH of 6.9-7.1 is considered neutral like Ca!
